Poza del Obispo

Poza del Obispo is a serene natural pool with crystal-clear waters, perfect for a tranquil escape on Puerto Rico's northern coast

November 1, 2015 Gabriel Pena

Nestled near Arecibo, Poza del Obispo offers a picturesque coastal escape that combines natural beauty with a sense of tranquility. This hidden gem is a shallow, turquoise pool bordered by rugged rocks, creating a sheltered spot where visitors can swim and relax away from the strong waves of the Atlantic Ocean. With its clear waters and stunning surroundings, Poza del Obispo is a favorite destination for those looking to unwind and enjoy Puerto Rico's coastline. Protected by a natural rock barrier, the pool provides calm waters ideal for families and those seeking a peaceful swim. The nearby Arecibo Lighthouse adds to the scenic appeal, giving visitors a glimpse into Puerto Rico's rich maritime history. Beyond the pool, the surrounding area features trails, rocky formations, and spots for picnics, making it an inviting location for exploration and relaxation.
